OCCULTURE Berlin | Demetris Navras
Offering Tarot readings on Saturday 2 November
Demetris Navras
Demetris is a dancer-performer, occultist and environmental scientist born and raised in Crete, Greece. His interest in tradition, witchcraft and rituals brought him to dance and performance. He studies the occult alphabets and symbolisms for more than 10 years and he understands tarot as an intellectual way to make sentences. He is focusing at the moment in Thoth deck which he find a well rounded study in the tarot imagery. Astrology, alchemy, kabbalah and Lacanian psychoanalysis are some of what he uses in a divination session.
