OCCULTURE Berlin | Letizia Iman
Dirt Pulse
Letizia Iman
What if the body, in a certain state of consciousness, becomes something else? Through Butoh dance I get into body and mind limits, in order to reach a point of total surrender. In this space the body explores his natural pulsion to the ground, the depth, the abyss.
Letizia Iman is a analogue photographer and performer Berlin based. She discovered Butoh dance in 2016 and then started to explore all the expressive potential of the body.
