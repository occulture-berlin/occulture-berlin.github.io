Kassandra the Kat
Kat is an Artist, Healing Facilitator, Astrologist and Anthropologist. She burns for Exploring the different Aspects and archetypal Patterns of our Evolution and its her highest Joy to offer her Reflections to Others, So that they may have a clearer Sense of themselves in Relationship to the wider Fabric of Existence. She is all about cultivating that Point where the Micro and the Macro, the Individual and the Collective meet, and the Relationship of that Polarity can be more deeply understood, as well as all the subtle and beautiful Layers in between. If she isnt going deep with someone about the Nature of the Universe, she is cooking yummy Food, swaying her Hips to juicy Soul Music.
