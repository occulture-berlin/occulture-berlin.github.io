Untied – Released
Charlyne864
Follow her along a path towards self-love. From a broken and crippled self, she recovers her capacity to allow herself to be vulnerable yet in control. Her eventual release from suffering and despair leads her to a deeply cathartic experience. Her struggle captivates the audience with ropes, needles and blood.
Charlyne864 fell in love with the Japanese art of Shibari while watching a performance at Torture Garden in London in 2009. Since then, she started learning the art herself along with piercing and body suspension. An early influence on her work was Meister Schmerz from L.A., with whom she lived in a dungeon. In Berlin since 2013, she has moved further into the realm of occult and spiritual performances and private sessions.
