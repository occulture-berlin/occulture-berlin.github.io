Offering Tarot readings on Friday 1 November and Saturday 2 November
Bernie Tarot
With patience, a kind ear and a soft voice, Bernie takes you on a Tarot session designed to bring clarity to your life. Since he was a teenager, Tarot has been his passion and helping people with his cards has shaped his whole life. Through his readings, he wishes to inspire people to see all the beauty that they are made of.
