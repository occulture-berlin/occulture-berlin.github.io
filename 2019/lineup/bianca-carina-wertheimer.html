OCCULTURE Berlin | Bianca Carina Wertheimer
Step out of the dark: Public Rituals and Interfaith Dialoge
Bianca Carina Wertheimer
My own path, my beliefs, my experiences, isn't this something so personal that i should not talk about it? And what if i am going to lose my job if my collegues find out i am a witch, druid, shaman...? I have walked that path for 19 years now and decided to step out of the dark a few years ago. I wanted to be visible, to be seen, to be accepted. Not only for myself but also for others. And yes that has changed my life. For the better. I want to share some experiences with you. Storys about presswork, interfaith Dialoge and Public Rituals. Its been a hell of a ride with ups and downs. But it was worth it. Let us discuss why it is necessary to do this sometimes nasty but also exciting job and how it could help not only you but your circle, coven, grove and our whole pagan community.
